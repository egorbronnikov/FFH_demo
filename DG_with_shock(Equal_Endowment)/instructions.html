Instructions

You will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. In this study, one of you will be Participant 1 and the other Participant 2. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role, which will be assigned according to a shock.

The shock is represented by a change in a Bitcoin value denoted USA Dollars. At some time you will be forwarded to a page where you can see Bitcoin value changing in real time. At [time] the value will be taken as a benchmark: if the last digit in Bitcoin value is odd Participant 1 will be hit by a shock, if the last digit in Bitcoin value is even, Participant 2 will be hit by a shock.

In the beginning of the game both Participant 1 and Participant 2 has {{ Constants.endowment }} each. After the shock is realised, Participant who is hit by a shock looses his/her endowment and the other participant has to decide how much he/she will retain. Then the rest will go to Participant 2.
