{% if session.config.endowment == 'equal' %}

You will be paired randomly and anonymously with another participant. In this study, one of you will be Participant A and the other Participant B. Prior to making a decision, you will learn your role.

At the beginning of the game, you are given {{ Constants.endowment_equal }}, while the other participant is given {{ Constants.endowment_equal }}.

The roles will be assigned as follows. When the experiment begins you will be forwarded to a waiting page at which there will be a [device: graph/random number generator] that generates random numbers. After a minute of observing the [device] at work, the procedure of generating new numbers will be stopped. The last digit of the observed number will determine the role of the participant:

- if the number is odd, Participant A loses {{ Constants.endowment_equal }} from what he had in the beginning of the game and Participant B loses 0;

- if the number is even, Participant A loses 0 from what he had in the beginning of the game and Participant B loses {{ Constants.endowment_equal }}.

The participant that stays with money will be enabled to share a certain amount (from 0 to {{ Constants.endowment_equal }}) with another participant. He/she will be asked to make a decision on the amount to transfer.
